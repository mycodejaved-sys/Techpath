Third Year CSE Roadmap

Your guide to the third step of B.Tech CSE journey

Step 1: Advanced Computer Science Subjects

  • Compiler Design
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning basics
  • Software Engineering
  • Theory of Computation

Step 2: Advanced Development

  • Learn backend frameworks (Node.js, Django, Spring)
  • Work with APIs and Databases
  • Build full-stack applications
  • Version control & collaboration (GitHub)

Step 3: Projects & Internships

  • Work on major projects (web app, ML model, mobile app)
  • Start publishing projects on GitHub
  • Do internships for real-world exposure

Step 4: Interview Preparation

  • Revise Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Practice system design basics
  • Mock interviews with peers

Step 5: Career Growth

  • Contribute to open-source projects
  • Attend hackathons & coding competitions
  • Explore specialization areas (AI, Cybersecurity, Cloud, etc.)
